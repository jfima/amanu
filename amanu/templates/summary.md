### PART 2: Executive Summary
- **Header**: `# Executive Summary` (or equivalent in audio language).
- **Format**: Concise, high-level overview for quick reading.
- **CRITICAL INSTRUCTION**: The summary, key takeaways, and action items MUST be in the SAME LANGUAGE as the audio.
- **Sections**:
    - **The Gist**: One paragraph summarizing the entire audio.
    - **Key Takeaways**: 3-5 bullet points with the most important information.
    - **Action Items**: A checklist of tasks or decisions made.

**Style Guidelines:**
- Be extremely concise.
- Focus on value and decisions, not the flow of conversation.
- Use bolding for key terms.
